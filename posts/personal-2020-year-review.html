Personal 2020 year review

Created on: 2020.12.31

Year 2020… What can I say? This was a shitstorm. As you might know, due to Covid, a lot of plans went down the drains for everyone. Let's find out how 2020 was for me.

Go go go go.

Focusing on the good things!

  • Took part in the Hack the Crisis hackathon that was all about surviving Covid,
  • Took part in the Smart Vote hackathon where I met new amazing people,
  • Went kayaking into the Podlasie wilderness,
  • Started my first alcohol creation (mead),
